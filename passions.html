— My Passions —

Singing

I remember during 5th grade, my mom slightly bribed me into joining KAMA which stands for Korean American Music Academy. At the time, I had zero interest in singing. However, after a couple months of being inside KAMA, I began to notice that I was practicing singing on my own unconsciously. I realized that I felt differently whenever I was singing because it was an opportunity for me to express myself and delve deeper into my passions.

After being deeply involved with the KAMA community, I began to find new opportunities in which I could provide a service while practicing my passions. It just so happened that our church was in need of a lead singer. Without hesitation, I knew I had to take the opportunity. Starting September 2017, I began to attend the weekly practices so that we could prepare for the band performances every sunday at our church.

Here are two Korean songs I covered:

Everyday every moment song sover by Nathan Lee and two friends, Original by Paul Kim

Love song sover by Nathan Lee, Original by Lim Jae Bum

Weightlifing

It was my sophomore year in highschool and I recently got into tennis with a couple of my friends. We were all on the tennis team together and practiced almost daily at the local park. I distincttly remember one of my friends' dad decided to take me and my friends to the YMCA that was around a 3 minute drive from the park. I did not have a membership so they just snuck me in. I remember laying on a bench and struggled to bench 65 pounds. However, even though I struggled to lift the weights, the adrenaline that I had while lifting and the soreness after inspired me to come back the next day,

After a couple months of consistent weight training, I began to see massive changes in my body and that raised my self-esteem and confidence. Prior to working out, I always had issues with body dysmorphia and weight. This new endeavor and passions that I discovered has laid the foundatioon for the healthy lifestyle that I live today.
